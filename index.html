Personal User Manual

My Style:

  • When I am in a busy environment, I have a hard time focusing on complicated tasks and often procrastinate.
  • When I am able to work alone, I can focus better and get things done.
  • I appreciate respect, it helps me to show respect in return.

What I Value:

  • I value music a lot because it helps me to focus and get things done.
  • I value and respect people that are humble.

What I Don't Have Patience For:

  • I do have patience for disrespectful criticism, it doesn't help anybody.

How to Best Communicate With Me:

  • Text messaging is the quickest way to reach me.
  • I appreciate honesty, I like to know when I am doing something wrong.

What People Misunderstand About Me:

  • I am usually very reserved, especially with people I do not know very well, but given time I am a little more open.

How To Help Me:

  • People can help me by sharing their perspective of problems I am having with a task so I can have more to go off of to make a decision.
